# How CGPA is Calculated?

CGPA or cumulative grade point average is calculated by taking the weighted average of the grade points obtained in all the courses taken by the student. The weightage of each course is the number of credits assigned to it. Project CGPA is calculated similarly but only using Project Courses.

# How to Use?

The application is fairly simple to use. You can start by clicking on the Start button on the home page. You will be asked to select your current level. After that you will be asked to enter your grades in the courses you have completed. You can leave the grade as '-' if you have not completed the course. You can remove the grade using the button. For students who completed projects as part of the theory itself in initial terms they should keep the projects grade empty for accurate result. Your project CGPA depends only on the project courses you have completed separately. After entering the grades you will be shown your CGPA.

# Data Collection Policy

This application uses cookies to store your session id. This is used to identify you as a user and to store your data. The data is stored in the server and is not shared with anyone. The data is deleted after 365 days of inactivity. You can delete your data by clicking on the Delete Data button.
