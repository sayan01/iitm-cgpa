Support IITM-CGPA

This calculator is a free, not-for-profit tool built for fellow IITM BS students.

Why Donate?

IITM-CGPA is built and maintained as a free service for students of the IITM BS programme. It is not affiliated with IITM and earns no revenue. There are no ads, no trackers, and no paywalls — just a tool that does one thing and does it well.

Keeping it online costs real money:

  • Cloud hosting and database servers
  • Domain renewal each year
  • Time spent on updates as the curriculum evolves

If this tool saved you time or made your life a little easier, a small contribution helps keep it running for the next batch of students. Every rupee goes directly toward hosting and upkeep.
